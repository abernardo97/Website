About Me

Hi. I'm Alexia Bernardo.

I attended my first anime convention, Tora-Con. Not as a casual attendee - I decided to join the staff running the convention despite never attending one before.

While I was at the staff meetings, I wondered how I’d be able to contribute anything. But despite the interesting email requests, angry attendees, and the occasional use of a first aid kit, I kept going on as a staff member. I never thought I’d get that much interaction with attendees, but I’d enjoy the experience.

I’m well-versed in communication, planning and development, and team leadership. If you’re interested in how I was the project manager of seven different projects in my third semester in college, please send an email my way.
