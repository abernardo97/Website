About Me

Hi. I'm Alexia Bernardo.

I’m a designer fueled by compassion, curiosity, and interest for interactive design. I’m currently a student at the Rochester Institute of Technology where I major in Game Design and Development Program that focus on the process of producing interactive experiences through games.

I enjoy being the bridge that brings artists and programmers together to ensure that art assets can be easily integrated into a game without sacrificing either the overall artistic vision or exceeding the technical limits of the chosen platform.

I’m passionate for innovative software that is a work of art. In my spare time you can find me playing retro games, going to hackathons, or attending local conventions.

I’m currently working on developing a game in Unity 3D called The Libyrinth where the player finds themselves waking up in their local library after it had been destroyed in a fire. A library that is being haunted by the ghosts of the former librarians.
