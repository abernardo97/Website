Project Description

Years ago, your local library burned to the ground in a horrible accident. You visit the newly rebuilt library and spend the day studying until you doze off. When you wake up, you find yourself in a twisted version of the library you once knew. Now you’re trapped with the ghosts of libraries past, and need to find your way out of ... The Libyrinth

The Process

Our team developed this game during a 5 week sprint on October 2017 for our Game Design and Development course at RIT. We have developed this game using Visual Studio 2017 in C# using Unity 3D engine. The 2D environmental textures was created in Photoshop and the 3D environmental and AI character models were modeled in Blender. Our team had won 1st Runner up at our major's department for the Interactive School of Games and Media and RIT in October 2017 for the initial prototype that was created at the time
